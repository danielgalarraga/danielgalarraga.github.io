An Analysis on FIFA Rankings

I worked on this project during the summer after my freshman year. Using Dash and Python, I created an interactive web framework that allows users to visualize the yearly average rankings of every association affiliated with FIFA, and obtain the top ten teams in each confederation. The framework also permits users to obtain the top ten teams of a variety of confederations. For now, this framework is not available to other users (this is something I am currently working on!).
